Shao ZHANG received her doctoral degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2025, under the supervision of [Prof. Ying WEN](https://yingwen.io/) and [Prof. Xinbing WANG](https://www.cs.sjtu.edu.cn/~wang-xb/). 

From 2023 to 2024, Shao was a visiting student at Northeastern University, Boston Campus.
Shao obtained her MSc in Multimedia and Entertainment Technology (Game Development stream) from [School of Design, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University](https://www.sd.polyu.edu.hk/en/) in 2020. Before that, she earned a B.Eng. in Industrial Design from [School of Design, Hunan University](http://design.hnu.edu.cn/) in 2019. During her undergraduate study, she was a member of [XiaoLab](http://xiaolab.net/) and was supervised by [Prof. Sheng XIAO](http://xiaolab.net/) and [Prof. Beibei ZHAN](http://ylsy.hnu.edu.cn/info/1353/9629.htm).

Shao's research focuses on **building agents for human–AI collaboration**, spanning multi-agent systems (MAS), human–computer interaction (HCI), and large language models (LLMs).  
Specifically, she is now focusing on:
- **Language Agents** for real-time simultaneous human-AI collaboration,
- Training **Agentic Foundation Models** for cooperative decision-making in multi-agent systems,
- Human-centered **Multi-agent Systems** for long-term deployment in real-world settings.
