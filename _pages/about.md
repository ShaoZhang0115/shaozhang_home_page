Shao Zhang is currently a researcher at [Shanghai Artificial Intelligence Laboratory](https://www.shlab.org.cn/).

Shao received her doctoral degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2025, under the supervision of [Prof. Ying Wen](https://yingwen.io/) and [Prof. Xinbing Wang](https://www.cs.sjtu.edu.cn/~wang-xb/). During her doctoral studies, she was a member of [SJTU-MARL Group](https://github.com/sjtu-marl) and [Data Intelligence Research Center](https://dirc.sjtu.edu.cn/#/). She obtained her M.Sc. in Multimedia and Entertainment Technology (Game Development) from [School of Design, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University](https://www.sd.polyu.edu.hk/en/) in 2020. Prior to that, she earned her B.Eng. in Industrial Design from [School of Design, Hunan University](http://design.hnu.edu.cn/) in 2019. During her undergraduate studies, she was a member of [XiaoLab](http://xiaolab.net/).

Shao's research interests lie in **agents and multi-agent systems for human-AI collaboration**, spanning multi-agent learning, human-computer interaction, and large language models.  
Specifically, she currently focuses on:
- **Agentic Foundation Models** for cooperative decision-making in multi-agent systems and human-AI collaboration,
- **Language Agents** for real-time simultaneous human-AI collaboration,
- **Human-centered Multi-agent Systems** for long-term deployment in real-world settings.
